Education and Experience

University of Plymouth

I'm currently working toward achieving my degree in Digital Media Design. This course is expanding my digital knowledge and applying my design instincts. With modules such as Creative Coding, Web Technologies and Strategies for Digital Art and Technology, I am becoming well-trained in industry-innovative approaches.

Devon County Council

Working and studying as a full-time Business Administrator for The Department for Public Health gave me my first public-sector taste for corporate design and content management practises. In aim to gain my admin qualification, I collected responsibility for almost all departmental websites and documents with task to make them as user-friendly and efficient as possible...something I eagerly worked on and improved drastically.

Exeter College

...Where it all began... Following 2 years of full-spectrum Information technology training, I gained a fundamental and crucial understanding of computer systems and their capabilities. I touched on areas such as Digital Graphics, Interactive Media Authoring and Human Computer Interaction. These taught me the essential principles of quality design and to focus on the user's experience.
